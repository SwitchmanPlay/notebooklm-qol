# Privacy Policy — NotebookLM QoL

Last updated: July 8, 2026

NotebookLM QoL does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any user data. 

- The extension has no servers and makes no network requests of its own.
- All settings (rename template, batch queue state) are stored locally in your
  browser via chrome.storage and never leave your device.
- The extension runs only on notebooklm.google.com and only automates actions
  you explicitly trigger (batch generate, rename, download, delete).
- There are no analytics, no telemetry, no ads, and no third-party services.

The full source code is publicly available in this repository and can be
audited by anyone.

Contact: open an issue in this repository.
